About Us

Clementina Aina was an Ofsted inspector with over 24 years of experience as an international freelance Education Consultant. Her school inspections and education quality assurance

Career span the UK, US, UAE, and the Cayman Islands. Previously Clementina has worked in Early Years and is the author of an e-book The Art of Communication, Reading, and Writing which was inspired by a memorable visit to a few of China’s most successful kindergartens. (99+) A Global drive to achieve Excellence in Early Childhood Education | LinkedIn

6C’s Was born from a recognition that the skills taught at school are not the skills required for the workplace. Five years ago, my daughter asked me a question that got me thinking ever since. Notably, it forced my deep reflection on the curriculum and some deep questions. Do students really understand how their learning connects to their career and how it fits into the rest of their lives? So, what exactly did she say? In the middle of her GCSE revisions, she asked me to provide an example of a chemistry equation I learned at school, and I simply could not recall. This conversation led to a debate on knowledge acquisition and ‘career preparedness’ The insights I had from curriculum framework reviews in the UK, USA, Cayman Islands, and UAE, inequalities witnessed (despite young peoples’ abundance of talent and creativity) strengthened my resolve to do something about it.

The idea for 6C’s came during a lightbulb moment at one of University College London’s Enterprise and Innovations business workshops. I suddenly had an idea that I could make better use of a presentation made six years ago for a GESS Exhibition held in Dubai (which was organized by the British Education Suppliers Association in partnership with the Department of International Trade).

6C’s Goal is to harness technology to equip students with the skills required for industries of the future. Our global community keeps students connected with like-minded peers with the common goal to steer a career reform that reflects their aspirations for effective, rewarding, and enjoyable careers. With 6C’s support, students channel their skills into the fulfilment of purpose! (And a generous size of the national cake!)
